The **Orceum Store** ([https://orceum.com/store](https://orceum.com/store)) is where users discover and install apps, similar to a traditional App Store. Your app's status controls its visibility, and your `description` drives its discoverability.

Users browse the Store visually — there are no API calls required to search for or install apps.

---

## App Status Lifecycle

Every app starts in a `PENDING` state after creation:

```text
PENDING → ACTIVE → SUSPENDED / ARCHIVED
```

| Status | Visible in Store | Installable | Description |
|--------|------------------|-------------|-------------|
| `PENDING` | No | Internal only | Just registered, not yet reviewed or published |
| `ACTIVE` | Yes | Yes | Published and available to all Orceum users |
| `SUSPENDED` | No | No | Temporarily disabled by a platform admin |
| `ARCHIVED` | No | No | Permanently removed from the store |

<Note>
Status transitions are managed via the **Orceum Developer Studio**. When your app is ready, you can submit it for publication to move it to `ACTIVE`.
</Note>

## Searching the Store

Users explore the Orceum Store by category, featured collections, or keyword search. Orceum's search engine matches user queries against your app's `name` and `description`.

### Optimizing for Discovery

Orceum's AI-powered search considers your app's description and action names when ranking results. To rank well:

1. **Use specific keywords** in your description — include tool names, task types, and related service names.
2. **Be concise but descriptive** — keep your description under 200 characters for the best UI presentation.
3. **Use distinct action names** — each action that appears in search results adds keyword surface area for discovery.

See [AI Summary & Discoverability](/configuration/ai-summary) for in-depth guidance on writing effective descriptions.

## App Analytics

You don't need to make API calls to track your app's performance. You can view aggregated statistics directly in the **Analytics** tab of the Developer Studio:

- **Active Installs**: Number of users who currently have your app installed.
- **Total Installs**: All-time installation count.
- **Uninstalls**: Users who have removed your app in the last 30 days.
- **Action Calls**: Total number of times your app was invoked in the last 30 days.

## Managing Your App

All app management operations are performed visually in the **Orceum Developer Studio**:

- **Update Metadata**: Edit your name, description, or configuration settings.
- **View Your Apps**: See a dashboard of all apps you own.
- **Regenerate Manifest**: For MCP apps, refresh your tool discovery with a single click.
- **Archive App**: Hide your app from the Store without permanently deleting it.

<Warning>
Deleting an app from the Developer Studio immediately removes it from the Orceum Store and terminates all active installations. Users lose access with no warning. We recommend **Archiving** your app instead of deleting it, in case you need to restore it later.
</Warning>
